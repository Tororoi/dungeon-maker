  1. Draw a map on the small upper canvas using the colors on the right.
  2. Press 'Generate Map' and the walls and skeletons will be placed according to your drawing.
  3. Move the player using the arrow keys.
  4. Skeletons will follow the player while it's on the canvas.
  5. Press 'space' when the circle appears to kill or revive the skeleton.
